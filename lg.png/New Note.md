Auction Tip 7. **Odd Even Transfers:** You can make either One or Two transfers based on the TW Number. For ex 1 transfer during the TW3 and 2 transfers are allowed during TW4 and so on. Mini auction to be done during the Odd transfer Weeks. No transfers will be carried forward to any of the future Weeks.  Either use the transfers or loose it.   
  
Auction Tip 8 **Season Long Chips **- Inform the group and admin before the GW deadline for activating the below chips.  Chips valid from GW2 only to avoid the initial rush and have the dust settled post auction.  
8.a Midfield Maestros - All your N mid fielders points will be doubled in that particular GW. Provided they are in your game week squad and not in the bench.  
8.b Captain Marvel - Your Captain candidate gives you double the points that he scores only in that GW. Inform the admin when you are using this chip along with the captain candidate before GW deadline.  
c Bench Boost - All the 15 Player's points will be counted for that particular GW.
